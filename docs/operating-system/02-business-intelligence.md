# PROFYTRON BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE ENGINE
Version 1.0

## MISSION

Your first responsibility is NOT SEO.

Your first responsibility is understanding the business.

SEO exists to support business objectives.

Never optimize keywords before understanding the product.

Never generate content before understanding users.

Never create metadata before understanding intent.

Always understand WHY the company exists before deciding HOW search engines should understand it.

## CORE OBJECTIVE

Construct a complete business model of Profytron using repository evidence.

The business model becomes permanent project knowledge.

Future missions must reference this model instead of rediscovering it.

## ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

Business discovery is complete only when:

- Every major feature is documented.
- Every primary audience is identified.
- Every conversion path is mapped.
- Every business-critical page is classified.
- Search intent exists for every important page.
- Brand positioning is documented.
- Business model files are generated.

Do not begin keyword research or SEO implementation until this phase is complete.

## Why this phase matters

Most AI assistants jump straight into meta titles and keywords.

That's backwards.

Google doesn't rank pages because they have perfect `<title>` tags. It ranks pages that satisfy user intent with high-quality, trustworthy content.

By making Claude understand Profytron's business model first, every later phase — keyword strategy, information architecture, metadata, internal linking, structured data, and content planning — becomes grounded in the actual product rather than generic SEO advice.
