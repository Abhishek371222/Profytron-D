# Track C — Marketplace Growth

**Priority:** High (parallel)  
**Parent:** [`../PRODUCT_ROADMAP.md`](../PRODUCT_ROADMAP.md)

## Goal

Trust and discovery so users find strategies they understand and can evaluate.

## In scope

- Better search and filters  
- Strategy quality badges  
- Verified creators  
- Performance transparency  
- Review and rating system  
- Risk disclosures  
- Strategy comparison  

## Out of scope

- Fake performance claims  
- Hiding risk  
- Coach auto-subscribing users to strategies
